FitPlan

A local-first gym training app. Generate a plan, log your sets, track progression — all on your phone, without an account and without a network.

What it does

  • Generates training plans from your goal, equipment, and schedule.
  • Logs sets and reps with a built-in rest timer.
  • Tracks personal records and estimated 1RM over time.
  • Suggests working weights based on your recent sessions.
  • Keeps your full history even when you change goals.

Privacy

FitPlan does not collect any personal data. Your workouts, body weight, and progress are stored only on your device. There is no account, no sign-up, no analytics, and no ads.
