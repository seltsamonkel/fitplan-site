Support

We read every message. For bug reports, feature requests, or general questions, email us at:

info@asssk.me

When reporting a bug, please include

  • Your device model and operating system version (for example, iPhone 15, iOS 18.2, or Pixel 8, Android 14).
  • The version of FitPlan, shown in Settings.
  • A short description of what you were doing when the problem happened.
  • What you expected to happen, and what actually happened.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if possible.

This kind of detail helps us reproduce and fix issues much faster. Thank you.

Privacy reminder

FitPlan stores your data only on your device. If you uninstall the App before contacting us, your workout history cannot be recovered. See our Privacy Policy for details.
